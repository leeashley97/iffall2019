Kimchi Fried Rice

SERVINGS: 1-2 PPL

PREP TIME: 10 MIN

COOK TIME: 10 MIN

Ingredients

• white rice 300g

• kimchi 100g

• spam 80g

• carrot 30g

• sugar 1tsp

• chopped leek 2tbsp

• oyster sauce 1tbsp

• minced garlic 1tbsp

• sesame oil 1tbsp

• 1 fried egg

Directions

  • 1

    Chop kimchi into small pieces to the size of your preference

  • 2

    Dice up spam, chop carrot and leek for preparation

  • 3

    Fry your egg in advance

  • 4

    Stir-fry minced garlic, leek, and carrot together in a heated fry pan with enough amount of cooking oil

  • 5

    Stir-fry diced spam and kimchi together with the cooked vegetables

  • 6

    Add in sugar and oyster sauce; make sure to stir so all ingredients could be well mixed

  • 7

    Once the sauces are mixed into the ingredients, add in rice and stir again

  • 8

    Add a tablespoon of sesame oil and stir again

  • 9

    Finally, place into a clean plate and add prepared fried egg on top

Enjoy!
